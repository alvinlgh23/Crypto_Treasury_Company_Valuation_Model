# Crypto Treasury Company Valuation Model

A decision-support tool to evaluate crypto treasury companies 
(e.g., MSTR, MARA, BMNR) using NAV, yield, and mispricing signals.

---

## 🔍 What this does

This tool analyzes companies holding crypto assets and answers:

- Is the stock trading at a premium or discount to its assets?
- Is the yield sufficient to justify the valuation?
- Is there a real trading edge?

---

## ⚡ Example Output
MSTR — HOLD

Summary:
Price is close to asset value, with no yield advantage.

Suggested Approach:
Wait for better entry point


---

## 🧠 Model Philosophy

> Not all good assets are good trades.

This model focuses on **mispricing and edge**, not just valuation.

Most outputs will be "HOLD" — this is intentional.

---

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

This is a simplified model and does not include:

- Market sentiment
- Macro conditions
- Company-specific risks

Use for research purposes only.
